## Data Cleaning & Validation Macro

### Problem
Business datasets frequently contain duplicated records, inconsistent formats,
missing values, and invalid entries, making analysis unreliable and time-consuming.

### Solution
Developed an Excel VBA macro that automatically:
- Removes duplicated records
- Standardizes date and numeric formats
- Identifies missing or invalid values
- Applies validation rules
- Generates a clean and analysis-ready dataset

### Tools & Techniques
- Excel VBA
- Data validation rules
- Conditional checks
- Automated workflows

### Business Impact
- Significant reduction in manual data preparation time
- Improved data accuracy and consistency
- Increased reliability of downstream analysis and reports
